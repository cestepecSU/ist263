Recipes

Moscow Mule

Throughout the year I have always loved getting a Moscow Mule at the bars I go to.
Since the bars are closed this is definitely the best recipe to still enjoy a great Moscow Mule.
The ginger taste that hits the back of your throat is incredibly refreshing.
I also like to add more lime to the recipe below.
I hope you enjoy making this!
Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 fluid ounced vodka
  • 1/2 fluid ounce lime juice
  • ice cubes
  • 1/2 cup ginger beer
  • 1 lime wedge for garnish
Moscow Mule Instructions
Step 1- Pour vodka and lime juice into a mug; add ice cubes and ginger beer. Stir to combine.
Step 2- Drop a lime wedge into the mug for garnish.
The ginger beer I love is the Triple Ginger Beer Brew from Trader Joe's its very good. Enjoy! Moscow Mule recipe
